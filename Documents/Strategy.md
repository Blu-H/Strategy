# Blu-H Public Strategy & Operating Principles
Draft – for public sharing

## 1. Why Blu-H Exists

Blu-H is a science-first, public-interest organisation focused on improving flood risk awareness and decision-making through responsible use of Earth observation, data science, and predictive intelligence.

While advances in satellite data, modelling, and analytics have accelerated rapidly, their translation into timely, trusted, and operational decision support remains uneven—particularly in vulnerable and disaster-prone regions. Blu-H exists to close this gap.

Our mission is to ensure that scientific innovation in flood monitoring is deployed responsibly, transparently, and in service of public good, rather than commercial extraction or closed systems.

## 2. Operating Principles

### 2.1 Science before solutions
Blu-H operates as an open scientific organisation. Methods, assumptions, validation approaches, and known limitations are documented and shared wherever possible.

### 2.2 Decisions, not dashboards
Blu-H focuses on enabling better decisions under uncertainty, particularly for governments, responders, and public institutions operating in time-critical flood contexts.

### 2.3 Public interest by design
Blu-H does not optimise for commercial exploitation of humanitarian or disaster-related data.

### 2.4 Safeguarding and dignity
Blu-H recognises that disaster and crisis contexts amplify vulnerability. Safeguarding principles apply across all activities, including remote, digital, and data-driven engagement.

## 3. How Blu-H Works

### 3.1 Open technical infrastructure
Blu-H uses open repositories and transparent documentation to manage scientific and technical work.

### 3.2 Clear separation of roles
Blu-H maintains a clear separation between governance, advisory, and operational functions.

### 3.3 No field deployment
Blu-H does not deploy individuals into disaster zones.

## 4. Governance & Accountability

Blu-H is governed by an independent Board of Directors. A Scientific & Strategic Advisory Board provides non-executive guidance only.

Blu-H aligns its approach with UN humanitarian principles, EU expectations, and the Core Humanitarian Standard.

## 5. Ethics, Data & Responsible AI

Blu-H applies precautionary approaches to AI, transparency of uncertainty, and human accountability in all decision-support systems.

## 6. Sustainability & Partnerships

Blu-H pursues sustainability through public funding, philanthropy, and limited cost-recovery services aligned with its mission.

## 7. What Success Looks Like

Success is measured by earlier and safer decisions, improved coordination, increased trust in science, and reduced harm.
