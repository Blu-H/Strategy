# Blu-H Strategy

This repository documents the strategic direction of **Blu-H ASBL**, a Luxembourg-based non-profit organisation.

It provides a transparent view of Blu-H’s mission, vision, strategic priorities, and medium- to long-term objectives.  
The repository is intentionally public to support alignment with donors, partners, and stakeholders.

---

## Purpose of This Repository

The purpose of this repository is to:

- Articulate Blu-H’s mission and vision
- Define strategic priorities and focus areas
- Document strategic choices and trade-offs
- Provide a reference for programme and operational planning
- Support transparency and accountability

This repository describes **what Blu-H aims to achieve and why**, rather than how day-to-day operations are executed.

---

## Scope

This repository may include:

- Mission and vision statements
- Strategic pillars and thematic focus areas
- Multi-year strategic objectives
- Roadmaps and milestones
- Theory of change or impact framework

---

## Relationship to Other Repositories

- **`governance`** defines *how Blu-H is governed and how decisions are made*
- **`programs`** documents *what Blu-H is delivering in practice*
- **`science`** documents *the scientific methods and evidence underpinning Blu-H’s work*
- **`operations`** (private) covers internal execution and administration

---

## How Strategy Is Developed and Updated

Strategic documents are developed through:

- Input from the Board of Directors
- Advice from Scientific and Strategic Advisory Boards
- Engagement with partners, donors, and beneficiaries
- Periodic review and formal approval through governance processes

Material changes to strategy are documented and retained for traceability.

---

## What This Repository Does Not Contain

This repository does **not** contain:

- Governance policies or legal documents
- Confidential operational plans
- Financial or fundraising information
- Draft partnership agreements

---

## Transparency

Publishing Blu-H’s strategy reflects a commitment to:

- Clarity of purpose
- Responsible stewardship of resources
- Alignment with humanitarian and scientific best practice
